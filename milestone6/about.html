About Our Company

Dixie Diesel Service has been serving Southern Utah for decades with trusted diesel repair and maintenance. We specialize in diesel engines for trucks, heavy equipment, and commercial fleets. Our goal is to keep your vehicles running safely and efficiently.

Our experienced mechanics are trained to diagnose and repair complex diesel engine issues using modern tools and proven techniques. We take pride in honest work, fair pricing, and quality service.

We believe in building long-term relationships with our customers by providing reliable service you can count on every time you visit our shop.
