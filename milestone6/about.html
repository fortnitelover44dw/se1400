About Our Company

Since 1956, Dixie Diesel Service has built a strong reputation for quality diesel repair and dependable customer service. What started as a small local shop has grown into a trusted business serving drivers and companies across Southern Utah.

Our team of skilled mechanics specializes in diesel engines and understands the demands placed on heavy-duty vehicles. We take pride in delivering reliable repairs that keep your equipment running smoothly and safely.

We believe in honesty, hard work, and treating customers the right way. That means no unnecessary repairs, clear explanations, and work you can depend on. Our goal is to build long-term relationships with every customer who walks through our doors.
